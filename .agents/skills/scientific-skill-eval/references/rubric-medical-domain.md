# Rubric: Medical / Domain-Specific Research

For skills with specialized medical research knowledge: epidemiology, biostatistics, oncology workflows, study design compliance.

## Score Criteria

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5/5 | Comprehensive medical research workflow support. Epidemiology-specific tools (cohort study design, case-control matching, confounding adjustment, stratification). PRISMA/STROBE/STARD/CONSORT compliance checklists auto-generated. Study design guidance (RCT, cohort, case-control, cross-sectional, diagnostic accuracy). Biostatistics integration with medical reference ranges, clinical significance vs. statistical significance. Oncology-specific endpoints (OS, PFS, ORR, DCR). |
| 4/5 | Good medical domain support. Covers 2-3 study designs with basic compliance guidance. Epidemiological concepts integrated. Clinical interpretation of statistical results. |
| 3/5 | General medical knowledge available. Can discuss study designs at textbook level. Limited compliance awareness. Generic medical terminology support. |
| 2/5 | Basic medical terminology recognition. Cannot distinguish between study designs or provide methodology guidance. No compliance awareness. |
| 1/5 | No medical domain specialization. Cannot reliably handle medical research terminology or concepts. |

## Score Translation

- **5** → 全医学研究支持+PRISMA/STROBE/CONSORT自动生成+肿瘤学生存终点
- **4** → 2-3种研究设计+基本合规，但自动生成能力有限
- **3** → 教科书级知识，无合规检查
- **2** → 仅术语识别，无方法学指导
- **1** → 无医学专长
