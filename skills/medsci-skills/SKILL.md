# Medical Science Skills

## Overview
MedSci Skills provides PRISMA/STROBE compliance checking, medical statistics, and clinical research methodology for medical and life sciences research.

## When to Use
- Conducting clinical or epidemiological studies
- Writing PRISMA-compliant systematic reviews
- Applying STROBE guidelines for observational studies
- Performing medical statistical analysis

## Process
1. Define research type (clinical trial, observational study, etc.)
2. Apply appropriate guidelines (PRISMA/STROBE/CONSORT)
3. Execute medical statistics pipeline
4. Verify compliance with reporting standards
