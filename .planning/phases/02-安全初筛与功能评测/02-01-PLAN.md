## New Two-Tier Scoring System

### Tier 1: Professional Depth Scoring (DECISION DRIVER)
1. **Function Selection** (Priority Rule):
   - Self-declared specialization → score that first
   - Multiple specializations → score the one with strongest evidence
   - No clear specialization → score the 2 functions with most concrete implementation
2. **Score 1-5 per function** (0.5 increments, 9 levels: 1.0-5.0)
3. **DepthScore** = average of selected function scores, rounded to nearest 0.5
4. **Threshold**:
   - < 3.0 → EXCLUDE (structured reason required)
   - 3.0-4.0 → CANDIDATE
   - > 4.0 → AUTO-RECOMMEND

### Tier 2: Supplementary Info (NOT scored for threshold)
5. **Integration Scores** (5 dimensions, 1-5 each): 集成难度, MCP兼容, 冲突风险, 维护成本, 上下文依赖
6. **Coverage Description** (DESCRIPTIVE, not scored):
   - Coverage_Stages: literature/analysis/writing/submission
   - Coverage_Description: 2-3 sentences

### Security (Independent Veto)
7. **4 checks**: 数据安全, 权限范围, 网络请求, 依赖来源
   - Any FAIL → VETOED + structured reason

## TSV Format (22 columns, from SCORING-TEMPLATE.tsv)
Repo | Domain | 数据安全 | 权限范围 | 网络请求 | 依赖来源 | SecurityVerdict | Function1_Name | Function1_Score(1-5) | Function2_Name | Function2_Score(1-5) | DepthScore(1-5) | Threshold | ExclusionReason | Integration_集成难度(1-5) | Integration_MCP兼容(1-5) | Integration_冲突风险(1-5) | Integration_维护成本(1-5) | Integration_上下文依赖(1-5) | Integration_小计 | Coverage_Stages | Coverage_Description
